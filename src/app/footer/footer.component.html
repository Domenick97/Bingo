Why use Google Analytics?

Like many other website developers, I use Google Analytics on https://domenick97.github.io/Bingo/. Google Analytics is software that fetchs data about my visitors (you) and with it I use the data to improve my site to better them.

What does Google Analytics record?

Google Analytics records what website you came from to get here, how long you stay here for, what kind of computer you're using, and much more anonymous infromation.

With this, all of my activity falls within the bounds of the /Google Analytics Terms of Service/.
